Who We Are

Northeast Florida's Tenant Improvement & Construction Experts

Impact Design Build LLC is Jacksonville's trusted commercial general contractor specializing in tenant improvements, warehouse construction, and ground-up commercial builds. Led by Jim Terrell — a Registered Architect and Certified General Contractor — we bring over four decades of hands-on commercial construction experience across Northeast Florida.

From tenant build-outs and office renovations to tilt-up warehouse construction and ground-up commercial developments spanning 5,000 to 350,000 SF, we deliver every project with an unwavering focus on safety, quality, and client satisfaction.
